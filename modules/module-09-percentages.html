9.1 Building Block Percentages

Instead of complex formulas, we break percentages down into simple pieces: 10%, 5%, 1%, and 50%.

The Building Blocks:
  • 100% = The whole number
  • 50% = Half of the number (Ã· 2)
  • 10% = Move decimal one place left (Ã· 10)
  • 5% = Half of 10%
  • 1% = Move decimal two places left (Ã· 100)

Example: 35% of 240

  • Break 35% into 25% + 10% OR 30% + 5%. Let's use 10% blocks.
  • 10% of 240 = 24.
  • 30% = 3 Ã— 24 = 72.
  • 5% = Half of 10% (24) = 12.
  • 35% = 72 + 12 = 84.

9.2 Fraction to Decimal Speed

Memorizing a few key fractions allows you to instantly convert them to decimals and percentages without division.

Key Conversions:
  • 1/2 = 0.5 (50%)
  • 1/4 = 0.25 (25%)
  • 3/4 = 0.75 (75%)
  • 1/5 = 0.2 (20%)
  • 1/8 = 0.125 (12.5%)
  • 1/3 = 0.333... (33.3%)

Shortcut for 1/n:

For 1/5, think "20". For 1/4, think "25". For 1/2, think "50".
